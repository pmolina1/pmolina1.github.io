COMM 205 - Communication and New Media

During COMM 205, I learned about all sorts of information that had to do with communication studies and new media. For example, I learned about the history of evolving media technology, the rise of web 2.0, and the social justice side of digital media like e-waste.


The Blog

For a final project, the class was tasked to do a blog about a topic that we liked or were passionate about. We were then asked to design and produce content for that blog using the website builder Wix.com.


One of my favorite hobbies is, of course, videogames, so I took the opportunity to build a blog where I could talk, analyze, and review videogames.

Check out my website live here!


This was one of my very first experience with design during my early college years. I was very excited to design even the smallest of details, and I spent a lot of time thinking about the theme, finding the right pictures, experimenting with organization of content, and writing blogs that were informative but fun to read. At the end, I was very proud of the design work I accomplished because I felt like I captured the aesthetic and feel or a videogame blog. I had a lot of fun designing and creating this blog!
